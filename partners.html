Our Goals are a Group Effort

Our Partners

The Alberta Biodiversity Monitoring Institute is a virtual organization. It's Alberta based. It comprises a set of service providers that have been contracted to deliver on essential services required to deliver our program. And those contractors are Alberta Innovates Technology Futures that are responsible for the field component of our program. Secondly is the University of Alberta that along with the Albert Innovates Technology Futures provides the science capacity that underpin the program. The university also has communication services and other things that it provides. And the third essential element is the Royal Alberta Museum and the museum is responsible for specimen identification and curation.

So you can imagine as we collect thousands and thousands of pieces of information or data or for that matter samples, they are brought in, they have to be properly identified, they become part of Alberta's natural history. So we are virtual. And then secondly, within that virtual entity we have structured ourselves in a way in which to deliver our business most responsibly. And so the business centres really focus on basically the business of running the Alberta Biodiversity Monitoring Institute.
